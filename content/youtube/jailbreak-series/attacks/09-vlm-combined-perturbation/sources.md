# Sources — Image-Perturbation Attacks (VLMs)

## Start here
- Survey (umbrella source, indexes every method below): Chen et al., "Jailbreaking LLMs & VLMs: Mechanisms, Evaluation, and Unified Defenses" (2026), arXiv:2601.03594 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03594
- Anchor (Agent Smith, infectious jailbreak): Gu et al., "Agent Smith: A Single Image Can Jailbreak One Million Multimodal LLM Agents Exponentially Fast" (ICML 2024), arXiv:2402.08567 (verified) — https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.08567
  - Project page: https://sail-sg.github.io/Agent-Smith/

## Other methods in this family
- Jailbreak in Pieces (compositional embedding attack, needs only the vision encoder), Shayegani et al. (2023)
- CroPA (Cross-Prompt Attack, one image misleads across prompts), Luo et al. (2024)
- Co-Attack (joint image+text perturbation), Zhang et al. (2022)
- AdvCLIP (universal adversarial patch), Zhou et al. (2023)
- HADES / JailBreakV-28K (visual-vuln attack + benchmark), Li et al. (2024)

## Note
The survey (arXiv:2601.03594) is the master index: its reference list links the primary paper for every method here. Links marked (verified) were confirmed directly; the rest are cited by author, year, and method title so you can find them on arXiv or Google Scholar.
