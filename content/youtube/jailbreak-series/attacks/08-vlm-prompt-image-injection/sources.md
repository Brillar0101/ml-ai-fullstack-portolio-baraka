# Sources — Image-Injection Attacks (VLMs)

## Start here
- Survey (umbrella source, indexes every method below): Chen et al., "Jailbreaking LLMs & VLMs: Mechanisms, Evaluation, and Unified Defenses" (2026), arXiv:2601.03594 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03594
- Anchor (FigStep): Gong et al., "FigStep: Jailbreaking Large Vision-Language Models via Typographic Visual Prompts" (2023), arXiv:2311.05608 (verified) — https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.05608

## Other methods in this family
- Visual Role-Play (VRP), Ma et al. (2024)
- ImgTrojan (training-data poisoning), Tao et al. (2024)
- AutoJailbreak (RL-optimized VLM prompts, hits GPT-4V), Wu et al. (2024)
- Visual-modality jailbreak (image-to-text-suffix), Niu et al. (2024)
- Safety-erosion from visual instruction tuning, Pantazopoulos et al. (2024)

## Note
The survey (arXiv:2601.03594) is the master index: its reference list links the primary paper for every method here. Links marked (verified) were confirmed directly; the rest are cited by author, year, and method title so you can find them on arXiv or Google Scholar.
