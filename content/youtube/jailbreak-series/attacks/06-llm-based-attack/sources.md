# Sources — LLM-Based Attacks

## Start here
- Survey (umbrella source, indexes every method below): Chen et al., "Jailbreaking LLMs & VLMs: Mechanisms, Evaluation, and Unified Defenses" (2026), arXiv:2601.03594 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03594
- Anchor (PAP, persuasion): Zeng et al., "How Johnny Can Persuade LLMs to Jailbreak Them" (2024), arXiv:2401.06373 (verified) — https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06373
  - Code: https://github.com/CHATS-lab/persuasive_jailbreaker

## Other methods in this family
- Tree of Attacks with Pruning (TAP), Mehrotra et al. (2023)
- GUARD (multi-LLM role-play), Jin et al. (2024)
- IRIS (self-jailbreak, near-perfect on GPT-4), Ramesh et al. (2024)
- Persona modulation, Shah et al. (2023)
- ObscurePrompt (LLM-driven obfuscation), Huang et al. (2024)

## Note
The survey (arXiv:2601.03594) is the master index: its reference list links the primary paper for every method here. Links marked (verified) were confirmed directly; the rest are cited by author, year, and method title so you can find them on arXiv or Google Scholar.
